## Problem
Many citizens are unaware of government schemes they are eligible for due to
complex information, language barriers, and lack of personalized guidance.

## Solution
An AI-powered platform that helps users discover eligible government schemes
based on basic details like age, income, state, and category.

## Features
- Eligibility-based scheme discovery
- Simple Hindi & English explanations
- Step-by-step application guidance

## Impact
Improves scheme awareness, reduces middlemen, and promotes digital inclusion.
